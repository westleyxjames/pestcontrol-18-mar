FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your first Sentinel assessment.

How long does the initial thermal scan take?+

A residential thermal scan typically takes 60–90 minutes for properties up to 3,000 sq ft. Larger homes, multi-unit properties, and commercial spaces may take 2–4 hours. Your technician will provide a time estimate during booking.

Are your treatments safe for children and pets?+

Yes. All Sentinel formulations are EPA List N certified and leave zero harmful residue once dry (typically 2–4 hours). We provide a mandatory clearance certificate after every treatment specifying the safe re-entry time. All products are tested for child and pet safety by independent third-party laboratories.

What does the 30-day guarantee cover?+

If pests from the same species and infestation area return within 30 days of your treatment, we return and re-treat at no charge. The guarantee applies to the specific infestation category treated. New infestations from separate entry points or different pest species are not covered under this guarantee but can be addressed as a new service call.

Do I need to leave my home during treatment?+

For most treatments, yes — we recommend vacating the treated area for 2–4 hours (the clearance period specified on your certificate). Our technician will advise you on the exact duration based on the specific formulation and areas treated. In most cases, untreated rooms can be occupied during treatment.

How does the Smart Perimeter Shield work?+

We install LoRaWAN-connected micro-sensors at all identified entry corridors — typically 6–12 units per residential property. Each sensor monitors vibration, temperature changes, and chemical signatures associated with pest activity. They report to our cloud platform every 15 minutes. Any anomaly triggers an instant alert on your Sentinel dashboard and to our operations center for review.

Can I cancel my monthly monitoring plan?+

Yes. Monthly plans can be cancelled with 30 days written notice — no long-term contracts. You keep all installed sensor hardware after 12 consecutive months of service. There are no early termination fees for cancellations after the first 90 days.
