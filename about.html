Our Process

The Sentinel 4-Phase Protocol

A repeatable, documented methodology that guarantees consistent, verifiable results — every single time.

01
🔍 Thermal Scan & Structural Assessment
Our FLIR-certified technician conducts a complete interior and exterior thermal imaging survey of your property. The scan produces a full heat-signature map identifying all active pest zones, moisture intrusion points, structural gaps, and potential entry corridors — all invisible to conventional visual inspection. The entire scan is recorded and time-stamped for your digital file.
60–90 minFull PropertyFLIR Certified
02
🧠 AI Analysis & Treatment Blueprint
Scan data, property dimensions, local pest pressure data, and historical treatment records (if any) are fed into the Sentinel AI Engine v3. Within 2 hours, you receive a comprehensive infestation profile: species identification, risk score (0–100), colony size estimate, predictive spread modeling, and a precision treatment blueprint that specifies exactly where, what, and how much to treat — and where to install monitoring hardware.
2hr ReportSpecies IDRisk Score
03
⚡ Targeted Precision Elimination
Certified technicians execute the AI-generated blueprint using our precision micro-injection system and EPA-listed bio-safe formulations. Treatments are applied only where the AI has identified active or high-probability zones — no broad spraying, no unnecessary chemical use. All work is geo-tagged, photographed, and uploaded to your client dashboard in real time. A mandatory clearance document is issued upon completion.
Zero-ResidueGeo-TaggedDocumented
04
🛡️ Smart Perimeter Deployment & Monitoring
IoT sensor arrays are installed at all identified entry corridors and high-risk perimeter zones. The Sentinel Perimeter Shield creates a continuous, self-monitoring barrier around your property. Sensors report to our cloud every 15 minutes. Any anomaly triggers an automated alert to your dashboard and our operations center. Quarterly technician visits, a 30-day re-treatment guarantee, and annual structural audits complete the protection ecosystem.
IoT Sensors24/7 Monitor30-Day Guarantee
